Our Solution: The Satviran Wearable

Our flagship solution is an intelligent, non-invasive smart wearable device designed to provide continuous monitoring for expectant mothers and real-time insights for healthcare professionals.

The Satviran Monitor

A smart wearable device (abdominal belt or patch) that uses advanced sensor technology to track key maternal and fetal health metrics. Data is securely transmitted for AI-powered analytics to offer real-time tracking and early detection of complications.

Key Features:

  • Real-time fetal heart-rate & uterine contraction monitoring
  • Predictive analytics for early risk detection (e.g., Preeclampsia)
  • Mother's heart rate, SpO2, and temperature tracking
  • Automated fetal movement (kick counting) and sleep analysis
